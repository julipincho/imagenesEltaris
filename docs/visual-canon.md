# Eltaris Visual Canon

Use this file as the visual source of truth for future generated images.

## Global Style

- Dark narrative fantasy portrait style.
- Painterly digital oil texture, not glossy splash art.
- Low warm candlelight and cold blue arcane accents.
- Backgrounds should tell story: windows, banners, laboratories, ruins, sigils, books, tools.
- Characters should feel grounded, worn, political, tragic or mythic.
- Avoid overly bright cosmic compositions unless the scene explicitly requires divinity.
- Avoid clean superhero posing, neon saturation, modern armor polish, UI elements, text, logos, watermarks.
- Preferred framing: vertical portrait or vertical scene, centered subject, dramatic but readable silhouette.

## Character References

### Orphin

Warm, mischievous older man with long dark hair, full beard, joyful expression, blue robe with gold patterns. Surrounded by symbols of chance: playing cards, coins, feathers, birdcage, hourglass, lanterns, twisting branches. Mood: benevolent chaos, playful divinity.

### Ophelia

Pale woman with long black hair, severe tired gaze, dark green/black clothing. Intimate candlelit interior, letters and wax seals, distant oppressive city through a window. Mood: restrained sorrow, hidden divinity, political danger.

### Maedra

Gray elf woman, white hair, sharp ears, round brass goggles, gold headpiece, worn laboratory coat and leather straps. One mechanical/arcane hand emitting controlled blue energy. Mood: cold intellect, guilt, forbidden science.

### Marvek

Older human veteran, short hair, gray beard, scarred stern face, heavy black Dominion armor with circular sigils, massive sword. Mood: disciplined regret, military authority, exhausted honor.

### Alera

Elegant red-haired woman, confident subtle smile, black and gold formal attire, holding quill and ledger. Background: warm amber office, stained glass with scales/gears, city skyline. Mood: political control, charm, calculation.

### Lathenka

Pale drow/elf woman, long white hair, pointed ears, heterochromia: one blue eye and one amber/gold eye. Worn blue robes, blue runes, dim stone chamber. Mood: fragile intensity, imprisoned power, free magic.

### Lucien

Elderly severe human man, bald or very short white hair, deep wrinkles, black and gold ceremonial robes, staff with circular Dominion symbol. Background: dark governmental hall, banners, low firelight. Mood: cold authority, fear disguised as order.

### Allen

Young hooded man, pale and haunted, dark worn clothes, chained spellbook held close, blue runes glowing on hand/forearm. Background: ruined city or shadowed Eltaris streets. Mood: fugitive innocence, dangerous memory, suppressed magic.

### Durmund

Heroic bald warrior, powerful build, dark heavy armor with Dominion-like circular sigils, fur mantle, blue magic in one hand, golden sword in the other. Mood: mythic protector, embodied conviction, human heroism elevated.

### Ankelath

Tall purple-robed tyrant/divine usurper, bald or alien head, violet eyes, two scepters, purple lightning and runic circles. Mood: domination, imposition, reality as property. Can be more saturated purple than the others but should remain painterly.

## Location References

### Durn-Vahl

Orc city carved into the fossilized body of a fallen stone colossus. Black basalt, bronze, bone architecture, rib-like bridges, skull-temples, red forge smoke, ritual hammer spirals, and cold blue free-magic cracks in stone. Mood: ancestral guilt, martial dignity, sacred forge culture, powerful civilization rather than chaotic horde. Avoid making it look like Rak-Thul'Kar: Durn-Vahl is more petrified colossus, bone, stone memory, shamanic ritual, and mythic orc politics than industrial goblinoid city.
